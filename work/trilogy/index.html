Hot Dogs is an animation about eating hot dogs and discussing the after life.

Hot Dogs is based on a trilogy of interactive animations made in 2018 and 2019.

Part 2, "Trampoline", is excerpted in In Here | Out There issue 3.

Screenings at
Florida Animation Festival June 2020.
We Like 'Em Short Festival August 2020.
Supernova Digital Animation Festival September 2020.
Blacksphere Film Festival September 2020.
Sweaty Eyeballs Animation Festival October 2020.
Animation Chico Film Festival October 2020.
