god is a ghost is a first person exploration game that takes place in a procedurally generated world. You can talk to characters in the game, search for answers to your spiritual questions and die.

Download for Mac or Windows

god is a ghost was part of procjam 2016

god is a ghost was exhibited as the Hygeniec Gallery in New London, CT, as part of the Connecticut College Symposia in 2016.
