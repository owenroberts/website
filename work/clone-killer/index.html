Clone Killer is an interactive installation that collapses video game aesthetics with a science fiction short story about clones. The user interacts with video and animation through shooting the screen with a hacked Nintendo Entertainment system gun. This points to the absurdity of this interaction as a way to advance narrative, as it is used in arcade games. The story involves a rivalry between a man and his clone.

Documentation at Wesleyan University, May 2007
